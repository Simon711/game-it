Industrial Talk by Mr David Chew One of The Founders of Pin Tea Malaysia.

On 14th June 2022, we invited the local business man Mr David as our speaker to deliver an industrial talk on Webex online paltform about his business journey in Malaysia, let's go through the event on that day.

  • 01 Details of Speaker

    Full name: David Chew

    Position:Director, Chief Business Development Officer (CBDO)

    Company name:Pin Tea Malaysia

    Company address:85, Jalan Perindustrian 4/KU8, Kawasan Perindustrian Meru Selatan, 41050 Klang, Selangor, Malaysia.

    Email: davidchew668@gmail.com

    Contact: 016-6960165

  • 02Interview Session with Mr David

  • 03 Q&A Session
    • Can you share with us what a typical working day is like for you?
      I wake up at 11 or 12 am, that's why I hate the 9 am-5am job. I usually ship breakfast and go to the office. Because I am the chief of business development, my main task is to search for creative ideas, what and how collaboration with other company, make Pin Tea more exposed. Usually I sleep at 2 or 3 am, sometimes at 4 am, due to scrolling Facebook, Tiktok searching for research on packaging designing. How to make the business much better, more powerful and stronger. Our brain is active at midnight.
    • How do you manage your stress?
      When I have stress, I don’t like to be distracted. I like to be alone a lot on my own. Because of a few things, I have a tea set to make tea. I just sit there alone. Just like a very quiet moment for me, then just drink some tea, make some tea like we called KungFu tea. I just relieve all my stress, maybe I can listen to some music. And one more thing, the favorite way to help me to relieve stress is to drive to Genting, as fast as I can. So whenever my favorite sport is not sky avenue or casino because I'm not a gambler. When I went to Genting, there was a temperature write 18° or what, from there we drove down the first corner and the first corner there have a blank spot, so I always stop my car there. I stop my car there then step down then the view is like I can look all the way all KL cities. From standing there I can release my stress, because when I’m standing there I will like stepping out from my city already. So I can see everything is very small. So when I have stress, what is the stress caused? When we have stress, that thing will enlarge, so negativity in your brain will like exploring already so every day your daily life will be distracted. So when I step out there, when I go to the hilltop, go to my favorite sport, when I look at the city, the city is so small, I’m thinking the thing that bother me that are trying to get in way is smaller than the city, so why do I care it, just go away. This is how I release my stress.
    • What's your favorite aspect of being an entrepreneur?
      I can say it is a challenge and my time, because the challenge in entrepreneurship is not easy, because the world is very cruel. We want to collaborate with the other company and the other company wants to collaborate with us are two different things. If we want to collaborate with other companies, they want to see our profile, attitude. If someone wants to collaborate with us, we need to look into their things, this is quite challenging for me, because not every company likes our culture, this is the truth. Most of them like us, because we are very free, we are young, we are lively, we can negotiate with the boss, we can also go anywhere we like. So, the aspect that I like the most is the challenge. Like myself, I like challenges the most, everything I'm doing now is challenging, so I like my job. The other aspect is time, because I can control my time freely, I can sleep all the way until 12 noon, then if I want to wake, then I wake up and go to the office. After work, I can brainstorm, meet, then I can go back home and spend time with my family.
    • What’s your most satisfying entrepreneur moment so far?
      One of the things I want to tell you is that, normally, we think doing business is very profitable. Businessmen can always drive a good car, that is nonsense I tell you, that is a lie. Instead, my crews are driving a better car than me, like GTR and Ferrari to my shop. So what is my expectation during my business? In this journey, I enjoy every particular moment. The best thing about entrepreneurship is I can observe my business growth. This is very interesting. The reason is no matter how big the achievement, for example, opening a new shop is one of an achievement, actually the achievement in the market is very small. For me, I enjoy starting a business by opening a shop. I also solve many problems during my business. Next, I also like to spend my time with my partner, because our working time is very free. Although we need to complete our tasks in time, every one of us works differently, not following company regulation. A company usually has constant meetings and writing reports, but we don’t need that. When we have a project to do with, we just need to go anywhere we want. Let’s say we want to go to some food restaurant, so we just go straight away and enjoy our trip. It could be considered 90% of the process that we are playing, and the left is the discussion. What we have done is we are learning while playing.
    • What's your advice to those fresh graduates that are thinking of creating their own business?
      In the different part of view, Mr. David thought that different sizes of business get their own way, not every startup business needs to have a huge start-up capital. People can start up what they like from a very small capital. For fresh graduates, they need to clear out their mind first, are they ready to jump up from their comfort zone or not. They should always have a backup plan and continue learning. The most important thing is to enjoy the whole journey.
